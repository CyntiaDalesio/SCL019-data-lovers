Hello, you curious! You have entered GHIBLI'S WORLD!

This is a website created to invite you to explore the fascinating world of one of the most famous Japanese animation studios in the world: Studio Ghibli .

Can you imagine a world full of happiness, fantasy, hope and environmental awareness ?
All this and much more is what two great masters, Miyazaki and Isao Takahata have managed to capture in their works from the creation of the Studio in 1985 to the present day; its Films are dedicated to "Those who were 10 years old and for those who are going to be 10."

Instructions for the trip to GHIBLI'S WORLD:
* * Open your mind and heart * *
